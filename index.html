Frequently asked questions

What is the purpose of the BTCETF token?
Bitcoin ETF Token (BTCETF) is an ERC-20 token that can be staked in our verified smart contract to earn passive rewards at a dynamic rate. It has been created to celebrate the expected arrival of Bitcoin ETFs in the U.S. financial markets.
Where is the value in staking?
Staking is an automated and decentralized way of locking up tokens for a period of time in order to earn more of those tokens, at a certain rate. In the case of BTCETF, the rate of rewards depends on how many tokens you stake, and the total amount of tokens staked by the community.
